Rita Cortez is a prominent Brazilian jurist and the president of the Instituto dos Advogados Brasileiros (IAB). She has been actively engaged in discussions related to China, particularly in the context of globalization and its impact on sectors such as Brazilian agribusiness. For example, she chaired a virtual debate about "Globalization and China," highlighting the importance of understanding China's role in global trade and its effects on Brazilian agricultural exports. The event emphasized strategic international relations and the need for Brazil to navigate global challenges with a focus on China[2].

Rita Cortez also appears widely involved in legal and institutional activities in Brazil, including leadership roles in the legal field and advocacy on various professional matters, but her connection to China primarily comes from her role in discussions about international trade and geopolitical impacts on Brazilian interests[2][7].
